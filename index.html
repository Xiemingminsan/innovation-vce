Our Solution

One cabinet. Every power source.
Automatic. Always on.

The Pre-Engineered Hybrid Electric Power Generator System integrates all energy sources into a single, plug-and-play cabinet with intelligent automatic switching.

Smart Energy Prioritization

1

Solar PV

Primary source

2

Grid

When available

3

Battery

Stored energy

4

Generator

Last resort backup

The system automatically prioritizes the cheapest, cleanest source first. When solar drops, it pulls from grid. When grid fails, battery kicks in. Generator only activates at 50% battery — then shuts down when clean sources return.
